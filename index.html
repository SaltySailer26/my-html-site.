Is This Legal? (Yes. Here's Why.)

Hootova is a referral tracking tool, not a payment platform. There are no kickbacks, commissions, or fees tied to your recommendations. It's simply a branded directory of service providers you trust—like handing out business cards, but smarter.

  • No financial compensation for referrals
  • No settlement service providers (we focus on general contractors, landscapers, mechanics—not lenders or title companies)
  • Full disclosure to clients that this is your personal recommendation list
  • 100% compliant with RESPA guidelines
