And yes, the money part

You're compensated for page traffic. Here's how it works.

Hootova pays you based on monthly visits to your referral page, the same way an influencer is compensated for reach. Not per lead. Not per transaction. For traffic, because traffic reflects the quality of the curation. The better your list, the more people return to it, the more you earn.

No kickbacks

You're paid for reach and curation, not for individual referrals. This keeps us cleanly outside RESPA settlement-services restrictions.

Fully disclosed

Every referral page includes a disclosure to clients. No hidden incentives. Your integrity stays intact because the model is transparent.

General service pros only

Landscapers, mechanics, electricians, plumbers. No lenders. No title companies. No home inspectors. The categories that don't trigger settlement-services law.

Your page, your standards

Compensation only matters if the list is real. You control who's on it. We pay you to maintain quality, not to push specific vendors.

Built-in disclosure

This is what your clients see.

A short, plain-language disclosure appears on every referral page. You don't write it. You don't add it. It's already there.

"I'm compensated by Hootova based on page traffic, which is how I make sure these vendors take great care of the people I send them. If your experience is anything less than excellent, let me know. I'll handle it."

Transparency is the product. Clients trust you more, vendors take the work seriously, and your reputation does the rest.
