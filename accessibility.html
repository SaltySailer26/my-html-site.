Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Our Commitment

Hootova is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually work to improve the user experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards to our website and services.

Standards We Follow

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at the AA level. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, language, learning, and neurological disabilities.

Measures We Take

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios between text and backgrounds
  • Alt text on images describing their content and purpose
  • Keyboard-navigable interface elements, including forms and modals
  • Responsive design that adapts to different screen sizes and zoom levels
  • Clear, readable typography at appropriate sizes
  • Descriptive link text that makes sense out of context
  • Form labels and input associations for screen reader compatibility

Known Limitations

While we strive to meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards across our entire website, some content may not yet be fully accessible. We are actively working to identify and resolve accessibility barriers.

If you encounter a specific accessibility issue, please let us know so we can address it promptly.

Third-Party Content

Our website may link to third-party vendor websites or services. We are not responsible for the accessibility of external sites, but we encourage our vendor partners to maintain accessible web presences.

Feedback and Contact

Legal

This statement was prepared in consideration of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act, and the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 published by the Web Accessibility Initiative (WAI) of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). This statement applies to the website at hootova.com.
